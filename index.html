SpinSasa — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ronsoft Tech ("we", "us") handles information when you use the SpinSasa mobile application ("the App"). By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SpinSasa does not require an account. We do not ask for, and do not collect, your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, or photos. The wheels you create, your options, and your app settings are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. Uninstalling the App deletes this local data.

2. Information Collected Automatically

The App uses third-party services provided by Google that may automatically collect certain information:

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App shows advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier, IP address, device information, and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads, which may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your device settings and region. You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your device's Google settings. See Google's advertising policy.

Analytics (Google Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used — for example, how often wheels are spun, which screens are viewed, and general device and country information. This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you. See the Firebase privacy documentation.

Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

If the App crashes, technical information about the crash (device model, operating system version, and the state of the App at the time) is sent to Firebase Crashlytics so we can fix bugs. Crash reports do not include the content of your wheels.

Purchases (Google Play Billing)

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment card details. Purchase records are handled under Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use This Information

We use the automatically collected information solely to: display and measure advertising that funds the free version of the App, understand aggregate usage so we can improve features, diagnose and fix crashes, and deliver purchases you make.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Automatically collected data described above is shared only with Google (AdMob, Firebase, Google Play) as necessary to operate those services, and where required by law.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Wheel and settings data lives only on your device — delete it anytime by clearing the App's data or uninstalling. Data held by Google services is retained according to Google's retention policies. To reset your advertising identity, use "Delete advertising ID" in your device's Google settings.

6. Children

SpinSasa is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, contact us and we will address it.

7. Security

Data stored on your device stays on your device. Data handled by Google services is protected by Google's industry-standard security measures.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves (for example, if new features change what data is processed). The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be noted in the App's store listing or within the App.
